# Wedding Celebrant Website Specifications

## 1. Project Overview

Create a professional, catchy, modern, and mobile-responsive website for a wedding celebrant (but also more) with light and smooth animations. The site should serve as a primary marketing tool, providing information about all services, and my personality, while also capturing leads through an inquiry form.

## 2. Design & Aesthetics

- **Style**: Elegant, romantic, clean, and modern.
- **Color Palette**: Soft and sophisticated. Suggested: Muted blush pinks, warm ivories, charcoal greys, and gold accents.
- **Typography**: A combination of an elegant serif font for headings (e.g., Playfair Display) and a clean sans-serif font for body text (e.g., Lato or Montserrat).
- **Imagery**: High-quality, professional photography of weddings, couples, and the celebrant in action. Images should evoke warmth and professionalism. Use images folder
- **Layout**: Full-width sections, ample white space, and smooth scrolling animations.

The website should include the following pages all in the same page with sections:

### A. Home

### C. Services

- **Other services**: Clear bullet points of what i also do: Tour Leader on the prosecco hills and treviso, event presenter also for book presentations or formal events.
- **Optional Add-ons**: I have contacts with many vendors and i can help you find the perfect one for your wedding like stunning prosecco hills places, deejays and bands. In particular i can provide you contacts to a mixer engineer for the sound system of the wedding and deejay with a jazz band.

### D. Process
- **Step-by-Step Guide**: A visual timeline or numbered list explaining the journey from initial contact to the wedding day 
#### Booking request
The first step is to check if I'm available for your wedding: you can easily use the form. If the day is selectable, it will schedule in my agenda a pre-reservation and I'll send you all the information within one day.
 
#### Let's get in touch!
The second step is, of course, to figure out if I'm the right person that you are searching for. We will arrange an obligation-free video call or meeting. Once you've decided that I'm the one, we can go on. Bookings are confirmed upon receipt of deposit. In case of legal wedding, I'll guide you throughout all the documents required for the process.
 
#### The preparation
My mission is to provide only the best experience, making the process enjoyable. I will take care of your wedding's speech that has to be a meaningful reflection of you both. You'll have access to your personal area where we can manage the rituals, readings, vows, and a few other details.
 
#### The town hall
In the week leading up to your ceremony we will have to go to the town hall bringing all the documents and signing a couple of papers. Of course, again, I'll be always on your side helping you with the bureaucracy. Also, we can do a rehearsal in person (of course accordingly with the other weddings).
 
#### The ceremony
It's your big day! I'll be there at least 45 minutes before the ceremony starts in order to ensure everything is ready. During the ceremony I'll be always there to handle all the expected things as well as what unexpectedly might happen. At the end, I'll be asked to sign the act of marriage and you'll collect the wedding certificate.

## 4. Technical requirements

- **Responsive Design**: The website must function flawlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices see tech-stack for more specifications.
- **Performance**: Fast loading times are critical. Optimize all images and code.
- **SEO Optimization**: Basic on-page SEO including proper title tags, meta descriptions, header tags, and image alt text. SEO is very important for this project so it should be the main focus focusing on wedding, Tour Leader and event presenter.
- **Accessibility**: Adhere to WCAG 2.1 guidelines (e.g., sufficient color contrast, keyboard navigation).
- **Security**: Implement HTTPS and basic security measures NO Backend configurations.
- **Cookies**: Include a cookie banner that allows users to accept or reject cookies.
